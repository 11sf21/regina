Regina was an alright city to grow up in. There was wide open wheat fields two blocks away from my house that were beautiful in the summer. We also lived under the flight path for the airport, so we always had passenger planes flying low over the house. There was also a lot of crime too though. I had my car window smashed in and stuff stolen. I also had my car totalled while parked in front of my house by a kid driving a stolen car. That sucked.
